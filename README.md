🧙‍♂️ Quiz Game — A Journey Beyond the Age of Rings

In a distant age, long after the tales of old have faded into legend, Frodo Baggins awakens in a world that no longer remembers the weight of the One Ring 💍.
Its power… forgotten. Its story… dismissed as myth.

Yet even in this unfamiliar era, destiny has not abandoned him.

✊ You take on the role of Samwise Gamgee, the ever-loyal companion, guiding Frodo through a new kind of quest — one of knowledge, wit, and perseverance.
Together, you must ascend through 25 trials 🏔️, each a step toward reclaiming purpose in a world that has lost its way.

But beware — the path is unforgiving.

Frodo carries only three lives ❤️❤️❤️:

The first two mistakes may pass like whispers in the wind 🍃…
But the third will summon the darkness 🌑.

Should you fail, the shadow rises once more — and the Dark Lord 🧿 claims victory, his voice echoing across the void.

Yet if you endure…

If you guide Frodo to the final trial…

A familiar voice will rise in honour and pride, speaking words once heard at the height of glory:

✨ “You bow to no one.” ✨
